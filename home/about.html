Our Story
Nearly all of the 250 million Indian drivers require car insurance, but the industry’s complexity and size create a massive grey area of coverage and pricing — drivers often don’t know where to start. We founded Koverup in 2016 to help drivers understand their insurance options and easily secure coverage.
Today, Koverup provides a simple comparison of more than 100 car insurance products from top insurance companies nationwide, giving drivers instant access to the massive car insurance market, and saving drivers hundreds of rupees per year.
