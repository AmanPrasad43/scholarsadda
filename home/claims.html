Reasons For Claim Rejection
Driving Without A License

No claim can be raised, let alone processed, if the accident happened with the vehicle being driven by you without a valid licence. An expired licence is as bad as no licence.

Influence Of Alcohol

This one is also self-explanatory. If it is proved that the mishap occurred when you happened to be intoxicated, there is no question of any claim being processed.

Consequential damages

Let's assume a certain part of your car (not covered by warranty) suffers damage. Due to that, another part of your car (covered under your policy) fails. Hence, not covered.

Policy Not In Your Name

If you’re buying a used car, ensure that the insurance has also been transferred to your name along with the registration certificate. The insurer is entitled to refuse the claim.
